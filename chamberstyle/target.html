Target Audience

The audience is people thirty years old ahead that work outside the city, travels more than one hour to their respective jobs and need to know how the weather is going to be to choose the clothes, if they need to carry an umbrella, how long the travel will be and what kind of transportation they will choose for that day. They use laptops, computers and mostly mid-range cellphones. The audience also are people over sixty years old women or over, 70 years old men or over, that travel every week to the center of the city to buy everything they need for their homes and they want to know the weather to decide if will go out that day, they will need an umbrella or if they work on their house to be prepare for the rain. They use mostly low and middle range cellphones.
